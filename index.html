Endurance Corner Cycling Challenge:

Ride With ZEAL Optics

Enter to win Zeal Sunglasses

ZEAL Optics is proud to announce an exciting new contest in partnership with Endurance Corner, world-renowned triathlete and ZEAL Ambassador Justin Daerr and Strava. We are gearing up to get you out and cranking on your bike for the next two weeks, and throwing in the opportunity to win one of six pairs of ZEAL Performance sunglasses as we inspire you to #ExploreMore from your bike’s saddle.

The competition starts Monday, July 18 and ends July 31. The winners of each category will win a pair of ZEAL Optics Performance sunglasses, designed and tested in the cycling mecca of Boulder, Colorado. ZEAL’s plant-based eyewear, like the city they're created in, are built for life outdoors, to help you push your limits, and tirelessly refined by athletes like Justin Daerr to ensure the road ahead is your only focus.

Rules and Regulations

To be eligible for the cycling challenge, you must:

  1. Join the Strava Club linked here

  2. Like both Endurance Corner & Zeal Optics on Facebook

Please note that the competition will only account for rides uploaded to Strava. No manual entries will count towards your total – i.e., all eligible rides must be uploaded through a device (Garmin, Timex, etc.) or logged using the Strava app or program such as Zwift.

Additionally, the winner of one category will be excluded from the other competition categories, thus six different people will win sunglasses. If a contestant ranks highest in both miles covered and elevation gained, he/she will win for the “Miles Covered” category and the runner up in the “Elevation Gained” category will be awarded the sunglasses for that category.

No purchase is necessary to enter or win. A purchase will not increase your chances of winning.
